Hi, I'm Eldrids,

I’m a 20-year-old Computer Engineering student currently studying at the University of Rizal System, Morong Campus. While I have always been drawn to creativity and the arts, circumstances led me to pursue this degree instead. Despite this, I have grown to appreciate the world of technology, finding excitement in designing and innovation. As part of my academic journey, I created this webpage as a requirement for my Web App Development course, handled by Ms. Crystaleene Jade Santos. This project is not just an assignment but also a learning experience and a creative outlet—allowing me to blend my technical knowledge with my artistic side. It has challenged me to think critically, solve problems, and refine my design skills. Through making this simple website, I have gained a deeper appreciation for the intersection of technology and creativity.

Honestly, I had no idea where to start or how to build this website—but here we are! At first, the thought of creating an entire webpage from scratch felt overwhelming. I wasn’t sure how to structure it, what design choices to make, or even how to properly bring my ideas to life. This webpage is like a digital diary, filled with my interests, my favorites, and the things I’m completely obsessed with. Whether it's hobbies, music, or random things that bring me joy, this site holds a piece of me. If there's one thing you’ll notice right away, it’s my love for the color blue—which is why I chose it as my theme. It’s calming yet deep, simple yet expressive, and in many ways, it mirrors my personality. Beyond academics, this webpage is more than just a project—it’s a reflection of my personality, interests, and passions. It serves as a digital space where I can express myself, combining my love for creativity with my growing expertise in technology. So, welcome to my little corner of the internet! I hope that as you browse through this site, you get to know me a little better—not just through what I write, but through the colors, the sounds, and the stories I share.
